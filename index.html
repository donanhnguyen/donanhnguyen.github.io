About Me

Greetings, thank you for visiting my page. My name is Don and here's a summary of what I've been up lately.

In early 2017, I started my first professional job as a technical recruiter at an IT staffing company, with no prior knowledge of the tech world. I immediately found a problem with my position: I didn't know how to talk to software engineers. In order to have an advantage in my position, I started teaching myself coding, utilizing everything from online resources and especially Youtube University.

By early 2018, I became confident enough in my coding skills to build my own projects. However, I became comfortable in my full-time day job, therefore I never pursued coding professionally.

Fast forward to 2023, I'm ready to pursue this career path fully and finally utilize my technical skills in a professional environment.

I've also been a creative writer since I was 15. My specialty is mystery/thriller novels with an internet/dark web twist. I've self-published 2 books in the Amazon Kindle marketplace.
